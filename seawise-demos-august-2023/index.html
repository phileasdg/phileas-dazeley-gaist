SeaWise (Pocket Pitch & Demos)

By Phileas Dazeley-Gaist
August 7, 2023

What is SeaWise?

SeaWise provides high quality data-rich real time navigation trackers and data dashboards for sailing events. Our navigation and visualisation tools empower our clients to build intuition, monitor event safety, and tell better data-driven sailing stories.

SeaWise is a unique SaaS venture specialising in data services for the competitive sailing industry. It aims to improve offshore sailing event tracking for professionals and enthusiasts by offering state-of-the-art tracking software for offshore sailing races.

SeaWise focuses on providing monitoring solutions for offshore sailing events, and addressing the shortcomings of current tracking software, which include inaccurate leader boards, limited features, lack of historical data, and limited weather insights.

Developing an all-inclusive sailing race dashboard, SeaWise incorporates advanced features such as real-time danger estimation, historical race and weather data, speed and progress summaries, and multiple simultaneous race overlays.

The SeaWise venture is currently at the seed stage and looking to build business partnerships and raise venture funding. Contact us directly here if you would like to discuss becoming a SeaWise partner.

The SeaWise commitment

Who is SeaWise for?

Event Organisers: Improve your event safety and event media coverage with near real-time detailed team position and weather tracking with monitoring assistance from specialised SeaWise machine learning tools.

Journalists: SeaWise is the essential monitoring tool for the sailing event reporter.  Keep your finger on the pulse of live sailing events using SeaWise's online charting, alerts, and filtering tools. Build intuition, and source reliable live and historical information for factual reporting .

Professional racers: Analyse your past performance and those of your competitors, answer factual questions about routing and weather using reliable information services, and plan your races using SeaWise software for an edge over your competition.

Yachting industry professionals: Track and interpret mined historical yachting data for industry, market, and advertising insights with SeaWise software and custom SeaWise consulting services.

Yacht racing enthusiasts: Follow your favourite events with detailed near-real time race information, weather reports, and media coverage.

Flagging critical moments (Safety example)

SeaWise leverages sailing event navigation and weather data to help flag for critical moments, for instance, safety crises. Safety crises are sadly too common in offshore sailing events, particularly during longer races. Let's consider two examples from once such race, the 2022 Golden Globe Race.

The Golden Globe Race is a full circumnavigation in solitary with no stops, no re-provisioning, no outside assistance, and no modern equipment, using astral navigation. It is a legendary race which has claimed many yachts. Most recently, the 2022 GGR claimed three. Among them, Asteria, captained by Tapio Lehtinen, and Puffin, captained by Ian Herbert-Jones will constitute the examples in this section.

The goal of this section is to highlight how SeaWise tools can assist in the monitoring, flagging, and analysis of safety crises at sea.
